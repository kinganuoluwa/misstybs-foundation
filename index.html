MisstyB's Foundation is the dream of a young lady who see education as a key role needed to help the future generation and build a better Nigeria for us all. This scheme started in 2018.

Sponsorship

The sponsorship program runs on sponsorships. The sponsors usually donate a base sum of #10,000 (ten thousand naira only) per student and of #75,000 (seventy five thousand naira only) per school (inclusive of teachers reward), sponsorship could also come in any amount or in kind (notebooks, pen etc.). Volunteers also contribute their quota in kind in terms of logistics, skills (publicity, graphics art, etc.).

Benefits to Sponsors

The sponsors of the Scholarship scheme would enjoy the following benefits:

  • Opportunity to touch a live and make Nigeria a better place for all to live.
  • Scholarship would be named after the sponsor that sponsors a whole school or more.
  • Sponsors logos will appear on all printings, flyers, books, vest, banner etc. used for the program.
  • The proposed website will contain all sponsors logos.
  • The volunteers T-shirts will be branded with sponsors logos.
